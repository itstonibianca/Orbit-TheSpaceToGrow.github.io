Inspired Literature


Orbit: The Space to Grow is inspired by the principles outlined in the bestselling book "Atomic Habits" by James Clear and the positive psychology framework.

Driven by a deep understanding of the personal journeys and struggles we all face when it comes to developing positive habits, our app aims to empower individuals in their pursuit of personal growth and self-improvement.

By integrating the concepts into our app. We hope to provide users with actionable goals and a supportive framework to turn their aspirations into reality.

Our Features

Stick to your habits

Our Habit Contract feature allows you to make 3 commitments to yourself to stick to your habits. You'll create a pledge, set a penalty for breaking it, and a reward for doing it – giving you a greater sense of accountability and motivation.

Schedule Activities

With Time Blocking, you can schedule any activity. Or, opt to try something new with Orbit. Each category holds suggested activities categorized by Energy, Recharge, and Build. All of which, can be directly plotted into your Google Calendar. This feature helps you stay on track, plan days ahead, and make the most of your time.

Measure your habit by consistency

Our Progress Tracker lets you measure your habits by consistency and track your streaks. You'll be able to see how far you've come and celebrate your progress through earning badges throughout your whole journey.

Get Daily Insight’s

Our Daily Insight Widget provides you with insightful facts and lessons every day. You'll can read quotes and facts based on positive psychology framework to keep you motivated on your goals.
